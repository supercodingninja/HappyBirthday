The Art of Giving

by F. D. Thomas, Jr.

No one has the power to waste my time;

And giving my time is Never a waste.

The hour come where I must give;

and emulate The One, Who gave.

In all I have, I will give my all:

I will give to All, to whom I am Called.

I will give without hesitation, or self-preservation;

and I will give without prejudice, or reservation.

I will give more than I take:

how well I serve others defines what I make.

My giving will be authentic, and influential;

However, open to reception is most crucial.

The Five Laws of Stratospheric Success, from The Go-Giver: A Little Story about a Powerful Business Idea is a business book written by Bob Burg and John D. Mann. It is a story about the power of giving. The first edition was published on December 27, 2007 by Portfolio Hardcover. Here are the laws: The Law of Value: Your true worth is determined by how much more you give in value than you take in payment.
The Law of Compensation: Your income is determined by how many people you serve and how well you serve them.
The Law of Influence: Your influence is determined by how abundantly you place other people's interests first.
The Law of Authenticity: The most valuable gift you have to offer is yourself.
The Law of Receptivity: The key to effective giving is to stay open to receiving.

Thank you for giving of yourself as Christ Jesus did.
